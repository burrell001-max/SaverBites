Baked Ziti Efficiency: How to Layer Cheaper Ricotta Substitutes Without Losing Creaminess

Ricotta can double the price of a pasta bake. Blending cheap cottage cheese with a single egg yolk replicates the same consistency and moisture barrier for less.

INGREDIENT UNIT LEDGER

SERVINGS: 6
INGREDIENT ITEM QTY UNIT COST
Ziti or Penne 16 oz $1.28
Cottage Cheese 1 cup $0.90
Egg Yolk 1 yolk $0.10
Marinara Sauce 3 cups $1.65
Mozzarella, Shredded 1.5 cups $1.35
ESTIMATED TOTAL MATRIX: $5.28
COST PER SERVING: $0.88

Preparation Protocol

  1. Prep the Ricotta Substitute: Blend cottage cheese with the egg yolk until smooth and creamy.
  2. Boil the Pasta: Cook ziti until just shy of al dente, since it will finish cooking in the oven.
  3. Layer: In a baking dish, layer pasta, marinara, the cottage cheese blend, and mozzarella, repeating until all ingredients are used.
  4. Bake: Bake at 375°F, covered, for 20 minutes, then uncover and bake 10 more minutes until bubbling and golden.
