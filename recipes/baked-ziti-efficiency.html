AUDIT ID: PASTA_ZITI_04

Baked Ziti Efficiency Blueprint

A massive volume expansion play designed for premium meal-prep yield. By engineering a structured blend of whole canned tomatoes, aromatic base vegetables, and affordable block mozzarella, we stretch a single box of dried pasta into a multi-day family asset.

2. Optimization Steps

01 / Under-Boil Parameters: Cook the ziti in salted water for exactly 2 minutes less than package instructions. The pasta must remain significantly firm, as it will absorb liquid and expand to maximum density during the oven baking cycle.

02 / Sauce Interlocking: Simmer your crushed tomatoes, sweated onions, and garlic into a tight reduction. Toss the hot under-boiled pasta directly into the sauce along with a 1/2 cup of starchy pasta cooking water to ensure the sauce perfectly binds to the interior hollow tubes of the ziti.

03 / Layered Thermal Melt: Layer half the pasta into a baking dish, sprinkle half your shredded mozzarella, and repeat with the remaining pasta and cheese. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 20 minutes until bubbling and deeply browned on top, setting the structure for clean slicing.
