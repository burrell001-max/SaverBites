The Cacio e Pepe Blueprint: Mastering Emulsification to Save on Sauce

Stop buying jarred Alfredo. Starchy pasta water and finely grated Pecorino Romano form a creamy, luxury sauce coat without an ounce of actual cream.

INGREDIENT UNIT LEDGER

SERVINGS: 4

Preparation Protocol

  1. Toast the Pepper: Toast cracked black pepper in a dry skillet over medium heat for 1 minute until fragrant.
  2. Boil the Pasta: Cook pasta in lightly salted water (Pecorino is salty enough on its own). Reserve 1.5 cups of pasta water.
  3. Build the Slurry: Whisk Pecorino with a splash of pasta water off heat to form a thick, lump-free paste.
  4. Combine: Toss hot pasta with the toasted pepper and cheese slurry, adding pasta water gradually until glossy and emulsified.
