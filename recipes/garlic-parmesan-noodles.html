Garlic Parmesan Butter Noodles Matrix

A meticulous, high-yield comfort dish broken down to the exact unit value. Rich, savory, and engineered for maximum cost-to-flavor efficiency.

SERVINGS: 4

Preparation Protocol

  1. Boil Pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to a rolling boil. Add egg noodles and cook for 6-8 minutes until al dente. Drain, reserving 1/2 cup of starchy pasta water.
  2. Aromatic Base: Melt butter in a large skillet over medium-low heat. Add minced garlic, stirring continuously for 1-2 minutes until beautifully fragrant without browning.
  3. Emulsify & Finish: Toss noodles directly into the garlic butter skillet. Remove from heat source. Shower with grated parmesan cheese and toss aggressively, adding dashes of pasta water as needed to form a glossy sauce coat.
