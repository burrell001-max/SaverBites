VEGETARIAN / TOFU

Crispy Tofu Hacks & Nutrient Matrix

A firm block of tofu, pressed and tossed in cornstarch before pan-frying, delivers a crisp shell for pennies. We audit the cost-per-gram of protein against chicken breast — tofu wins by a wide margin without sacrificing texture.

INGREDIENT UNIT LEDGER

SERVINGS: 4
INGREDIENT ITEM QTY UNIT COST
Firm Tofu, Pressed 1 block (14oz) $1.80
Cornstarch 3 tbsp $0.18
Neutral Oil 3 tbsp $0.45
Soy Sauce 2 tbsp $0.20
Garlic Powder 1 tsp $0.05
ESTIMATED TOTAL MATRIX: $2.68
COST PER SERVING: $0.67

Preparation Protocol

  1. Press the Tofu: Press tofu between paper towels and a weighted plate for 15-20 minutes to remove excess moisture.
  2. Cube and Coat: Cube the pressed tofu, then toss gently in cornstarch and garlic powder until evenly coated.
  3. Pan-Fry: Fry tofu cubes in hot oil on all sides until golden and crisp, about 8-10 minutes total, turning occasionally.
  4. Glaze: Reduce heat, drizzle with soy sauce, and toss quickly to coat without softening the crust.
