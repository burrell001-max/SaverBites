Lentil & Sausage Stew: Halving Your Meat Bill With Dry Lentils

Two links of smoked sausage, sliced thin, season a full pound of dry brown lentils. Dry lentils beat canned by nearly 60% per gram of protein once cooking liquid is factored in.

INGREDIENT UNIT LEDGER

SERVINGS: 6
INGREDIENT ITEM QTY UNIT COST
Smoked Sausage, Sliced 2 links $4.00
Dry Brown Lentils 1 lb $1.80
Carrots, Diced 2 carrots $0.50
Yellow Onion, Diced 1 onion $0.30
Vegetable or Chicken Broth 6 cups $1.08
ESTIMATED TOTAL MATRIX: $7.68
COST PER SERVING: $1.28

Preparation Protocol

  1. Brown the Sausage: Sear sliced sausage in a large pot over medium heat until browned, about 4 minutes.
  2. Sweat the Vegetables: Add carrots and onion to the pot and cook until softened, about 5 minutes.
  3. Simmer: Add dry lentils and broth. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer uncovered for 25-30 minutes until lentils are tender.
  4. Rest and Serve: Let the stew rest for 5 minutes off heat to thicken slightly before serving.
