AUDIT ID: PASTA_CARBONARA_03

Carbonara Hard Cheese Matrix

An elegant display of residual heat utilization. True Roman carbonara completely avoids heavy cream, using a rich emulsion of raw egg yolks, concentrated starch water, and rendered pork fats to achieve a premium velvet finish.

2. Optimization Steps

01 / Fat Rendering: Dice pork assets into thick matchsticks. Start in a cold skillet with no extra oil. Render slowly over medium-low heat until the bits turn crisp and golden, maximizing liquid fat release.

02 / The Tempered Base: Whisk egg yolks, the single whole egg, and your grated hard cheese base together into a thick paste. Mix in 1 tablespoon of cooled pork fat to stabilize the egg proteins against premature scrambling.

03 / Residual Emulsification: Toss hot al dente noodles directly in the pork fat skillet off-heat. Pour the egg-cheese paste over the top and stir vigorously. Add small increments of hot cooking water to let the ambient warmth transform the mixture into a glossy cream.
