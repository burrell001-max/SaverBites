Execution Protocol

  1. Pat chicken thighs completely dry with a paper towel (essential for ultra-crispy skin).
  2. Season both sides generously with your lemon pepper seasoning metric.
  3. Place skin-side down in a cold skillet, then turn the heat up to medium-high. Let the fat render out slowly for 12-15 minutes until gold-plated and crisp.
  4. Flip over, add the fresh lemon juice directly into the pan grease, and cook for another 5-8 minutes until internal temps hit 165°F.
