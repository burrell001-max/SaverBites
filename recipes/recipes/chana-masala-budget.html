Budget Chana Masala

Oil-soluble spice blooming extraction inside a deep onion and crushed tomato glaze reduction. A high-protein legume foundation built for absolute cost efficiency.

2. Optimization Steps

01 /The Browning Phase: Heat the oil in a large deep pan over medium-high heat. Add your finely minced yellow onions. Cook for roughly 8 to 10 minutes, stirring frequently, until the edges turn deeply golden brown to form the flavor bedrock.

02 /Lipid Spice Blooming: Turn heat down to low. Toss your cumin, turmeric, and garam masala spices directly into the hot onion-fat mixture. Stir constantly for 60 seconds. Blooming the spices directly in hot oil releases hydrophobic aromatic chemicals that water can't draw out.

03 /Simmer Integration: Pour in the canned tomato sauce and rinsed chickpeas. Bring to a gentle bubble, then turn down low. Cover and let simmer for 15 minutes, crushing a few chickpeas against the side of the pan with your spoon to thicken the gravy naturally.
