Savory Oat Congee Blueprint

An extreme volume expansion asset targeting rolled oats. By shifting oats from sweet profiles to a savory, umami-rich broth matrix, we expand standard starch grains to their structural limit, producing a comforting, ultra-thrifty meal foundation.

1. Component Unit Ledger

Green Onions (Scallions) 2 stalks $0.15 / stalk $0.30

2. Optimization Steps

01 / Aromatic Base Fry: Finely mince your ginger and garlic. Heat a splash of neutral vegetable oil in a pot over medium-low heat and cook the aromatics for 2 minutes until fragrant. Do not allow them to pick up dark colors or burn.

02 / Gelatinization Overdrive: Dump the rolled oats directly into the pan, followed immediately by your hot vegetable bouillon liquid and soy sauce. Bring the pot up to a rolling boil, then instantly drop the burner to a low simmer. Cook uncovered for 15 to 20 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the grain fibers completely rupture and transform the liquid into a silky porridge density.

03 / Umami Finisher Application: Ladle the hot savory porridge into deep bowls. Drizzle a precise measure of toasted sesame oil over the surface and scatter a heavy layer of finely sliced green onions across the top to introduce contrast crunch metrics.
