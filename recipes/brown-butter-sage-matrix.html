Brown Butter & Sage Matrix: High-End Flavors on a Pennies Budget

Browning butter triggers the Maillard reaction, creating a complex nutty flavor from nothing more than a block of store-brand butter and a handful of fresh sage.

INGREDIENT UNIT LEDGER

SERVINGS: 4
INGREDIENT ITEM QTY UNIT COST
Gnocchi (Store-Brand) 16 oz $2.24
Unsalted Butter 4 tbsp $0.88
Fresh Sage Leaves 8 leaves $0.40
Parmesan, Grated 0.25 cup $0.40
ESTIMATED TOTAL MATRIX: $3.92
COST PER SERVING: $0.98

Preparation Protocol

  1. Cook the Gnocchi: Boil gnocchi in salted water until they float to the surface, about 2-3 minutes. Drain well.
  2. Brown the Butter: Melt butter in a light-colored skillet over medium heat, swirling constantly until it turns golden-brown and smells nutty, about 4-5 minutes.
  3. Crisp the Sage: Add sage leaves to the browned butter and fry for 30 seconds until crisp and fragrant.
  4. Toss and Finish: Add drained gnocchi directly to the brown butter, toss to coat, and top with grated Parmesan.
