AUDIT ID: PST_ONEPAN_03

One-Pan Absorption Matrix

A masterclass in thermal conservation. By boiling dry pasta strands directly within your seasoned tomato water base, 100% of the extruded grain starch remains contained within the vessel, creating a naturally self-thickening pasta glaze.

2. Optimization Steps

01 / Parallel Asset Bundling: Place your dry linguine completely flat inside a wide skillet. Lay your thinly sliced onions, garlic, dried oregano, canned tomatoes, and cooking oil directly around and over the dry noodle bundles.

02 / Fluid Level Regulation: Pour in your boiling bouillon liquid matrix. The liquid volume must just barely submerge the dry ingredients. Bring to a rapid boil over high heat, set a timer for 10 minutes, and leave the pan uncovered.

03 / Stir-Induced Reduction: Turn and loop the linguine strands continuously using tongs while cooking. As the dry pasta hydrates, it drinks up the seasoned tomato broth, while simultaneously releasing starches back into the pan. This transforms the liquid into a rich, self-contained sauce right as the noodles reach perfect al dente structure.
