Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

At SaverBites, accessible from your GitHub Pages URL, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by SaverBites and how we use it.

Log Files

SaverBites follows a standard procedure of using log files. These files log visitors when they visit websites. The information collected by log files includes internet protocol (IP) addresses, browser type, Internet Service Provider (ISP), date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, and possibly the number of clicks.

Cookies and Web Beacons

Like any other website, SaverBites uses 'cookies'. These cookies are used to store information including visitors' preferences, and the pages on the website that the visitor accessed or visited.

Google DoubleClick DART Cookie

Google is one of a third-party vendor on our site. It also uses cookies, known as DART cookies, to serve ads to our site visitors based upon their visit to website and other sites on the internet.

Our Advertising Partners

Some of advertisers on our site may use cookies and web beacons. Our advertising partners include:

  • Google AdSense

Privacy Policies

Third-party ad servers or ad networks uses technologies like cookies, JavaScript, or Web Beacons that are used in their respective advertisements and links that appear on SaverBites, which are sent directly to users' browser. They automatically receive your IP address when this occurs.

Children's Information

Another part of our priority is adding protection for children while using the internet. We encourage parents and guardians to observe, participate in, and/or monitor and guide their online activity.
