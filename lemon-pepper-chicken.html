Crispy Lemon Pepper Chicken Thighs

Bone-in, skin-on thighs render their own fat in a cold skillet, giving you a crackling crust without a drop of extra oil. Zested fresh lemon beats bottled juice for real brightness at almost no added cost.

SERVINGS: 4

Preparation Protocol

  1. Dry the Chicken: Pat chicken thighs completely dry with a paper towel — this is essential for ultra-crispy skin.
  2. Season: Season both sides generously with lemon pepper seasoning.
  3. Render and Sear: Place skin-side down in a cold skillet, then turn the heat up to medium-high. Let the fat render out slowly until gold and crisp.
  4. Flip and Finish: Flip the thighs, add fresh lemon juice directly into the pan drippings, and cook until internal temperature hits 165°F.
