AUDIT ID: POT_CHICK_RICE_04

One-Pot Chicken & Rice Blueprint

A zero-waste structural emulsion. By preparing grain assets in the exact same pan used to brown chicken thighs, we force the rice to absorb every micro-gram of rendered poultry fat and pan fond, generating massive luxury-grade comfort metrics from budget building blocks.

1. Component Unit Ledger

INGREDIENT COMPONENT QUANTITY USED UNIT COST MATRIX TOTAL COST
Bone-In, Skin-On Chicken Thighs 500g $0.005 / g $2.50
Long-Grain White Rice (Dry Grains) 300g $0.0012 / g $0.36
Yellow Onion & Frozen Peas Matrix 150g / 100g $0.002 / g $0.45
Chicken Bouillon Substrate & Water 2 Cubes / 600ml Pantry Stock $0.20
Turmeric & Garlic Powder (Color/Flavor) 8g Bulk Stock $0.12

2. Optimization Steps

01 / Lipid Extraction: Season chicken thighs heavily with salt and garlic powder. Sear skin-side down in a wide, heavy pot over medium heat for 10 minutes until the skin turns deep gold and fully drops its liquid fat asset. Flip for 2 minutes, then remove thighs (they will not be fully cooked yet).

02 / Toast the Grains: Toss your diced onions straight into the hot rendered chicken fat and sweat for 3 minutes. Add the dry white rice and turmeric. Stir continuously for 2 minutes over medium heat to toast the exterior starch of each grain, which prevents clump formations later.

03 / Vessel Interlocking: Pour in your hot bouillon liquid, scraping up any browned bits stuck to the pot floor. Nestled the chicken thighs back down into the rice bed skin-side up. Cover with a tight lid, lower heat to a absolute simmer, and steam for 20 minutes until the liquid is fully absorbed. Stir in peas and let rest covered for 5 minutes before serving.
